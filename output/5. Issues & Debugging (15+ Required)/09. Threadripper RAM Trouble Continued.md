## Root Cause

- Threadripper systems with **8-channel RAM** are more sensitive to module stability.
- High overclock settings reduce long-term stability under continuous compute load.

---


## Resolution

- Recommended operational setting: **4800 MHz** (JEDEC standard).
- Lower frequency improves robustness for extended HPC workloads.
- Stability prioritised over minor performance gains.
