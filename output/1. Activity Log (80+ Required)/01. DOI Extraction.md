## Content


Objective:



To obtain the complete set of UK Biobank publications for downstream analysis of feature selection and methodological trends.



Process:


1. Downloaded the **Schema files** from the UK Biobank Showcase portal.
2. Retrieved **Schema 19 (Publications)** directly via the repository:[https://biobank.ndph.ox.ac.uk/showcase/download.cgi](https://biobank.ndph.ox.ac.uk/showcase/download.cgi)
3. Developed a Python script to systematically **extract DOI identifiers** and **PDF links** for each publication.
    - Total publications identified: **8,528**.
    - Successfully resolved PDF links: **2,905**.
    - Remaining **5,623 records required DOI-based resolution** due to missing or inactive schema links.

Reflection:



This step confirmed that the schema resource is incomplete as a single point of access. A total of ~66% of publication links could not be directly retrieved, underlining the need for a two-stage retrieval process (schema + DOI resolution). 



Next Step:



Automate DOI resolution for the remaining 5,623 publications and validate against CrossRef to establish a complete publication set.
