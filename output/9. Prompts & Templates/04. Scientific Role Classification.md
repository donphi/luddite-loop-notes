# Scientific Role Classification


## Content


```plain text
Classify the sentence into one scientific role category.


SENTENCE: {raw_sentence}

CHOOSE EXACTLY ONE ROLE:
- objective: States study aims, research questions
- hypothesis: States a hypothesis or prediction
- method: Describes methods, procedures, protocols
- result: Reports a result from this study
- interpretation: Interprets what results mean
- discussion: Discusses implications
- limitation: States a study limitation
- conclusion: States a conclusion
- recommendation: Makes a recommendation
- background: Provides background information
- prior_work: References previous studies

IDENTIFY SPECIAL SIGNALS (true/false):
- mentions_not_studied: Says something was "not studied" or "not examined"
- is_negative_result: Reports no effect/no difference
- contradicts_previous: Says "contrary to" or "unlike previous"
- identifies_gap: Mentions a "gap" in knowledge

OUTPUT FORMAT:
{
  "role": "result",
  "signals": {
    "mentions_not_studied": false,
    "is_negative_result": false,
    "contradicts_previous": false,
    "identifies_gap": false
  }
}

RULES:
- Choose only ONE role
- Base on sentence content, not your interpretation
- Signals are true only if explicitly mentioned
```
